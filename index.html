10,000

What is 10,000?

10,000 is a dice game where the object is to reach 10,000 exactly without going over. This is done by rolling six dice achieve certain combinations of dice that will give you points toward your score. Traditionally, this is done with at least two players, but for the purposes of this app, a single player's turns will be counted and shown to the player once the game is over.

Scoring

  • 1 = 100 points
  • 5 = 50 points
  • Three of a kind of 1 = 1000 points
  • Three of a kind of 2 = 200 points
  • Three of a kind of 3 = 300 points
  • Three of a kind of 4 = 400 points
  • Three of a kind of 5 = 500 points
  • Three of a kind of 6 = 600 points
  • Each number over three of a kind = double the points

Play continues until one player meets 10,000 points exactly. If 10,000 is exceeded, the player returns to their score before the start of the last turn.

For more information about 10,000, please see the Wikipedia Article.
